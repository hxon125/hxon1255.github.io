Who is she?

Avril Lavigne is a Canadian singer-songwriter, a representative figure of the pop-punk and pop-rock genres of the 2000s. She achieved widespread popularity with her 2002 debut album, "Let Go." With hits like "Complicated" and "Sk8er Boi," she became a household name and played a key role in popularizing punk-pop music. Her unique skate-punk style and rebellious image earned her the nickname "Pop Punk Princess." Her diverse instrumental playing and musical experimentation have created a powerful bond within the music industry.
