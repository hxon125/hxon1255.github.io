Let Go (2002)
A debut album that mixes pop punk and pop rock, featuring high teen anthems like Complicated and
Sk8er Boi.
Under My Skin (2004)
A rock album centered around heavy guitars and dark melodies, with many songs about loss, anger, and anxiety.
The Best Damn Thing (2007)
A cheer-punk style album full of fast-paced drums, bright guitars, and cheer-like choruses.
Goodbye Lullbay (2011)
An album centered around ballads and mid-tempo tracks, with many slow-tempo songs centered around piano and acoustic guitar.
Avril Lavigne (2013)
A mixed-style album that mixes fast-paced songs with an early pop punk feel, electro-pop, and ballads.
Head Above Water (2019)
An album centered around piano rock ballads and mid-tempo pop rock, with many songs featuring majestic choruses and strong emotional lines.
Love Sux (2022)
A high-energy album filled with contemporary pop-punk beats, cool guitar riffs, and collaborative tracks featuring a lot of artists.
